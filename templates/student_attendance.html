Troubleshooting Location Issues

If the app shows you are too far away but you are in class, please try the following:

  • Go Near a Window: A clear view of the sky gives the best GPS signal.
  • Toggle Location Services: Turn your phone's Location/GPS off for 10 seconds, then turn it back on.
  • Check Permissions: Ensure your browser has permission to access your location.
  • Restart Your Browser or Phone: A simple restart can often fix location issues.
